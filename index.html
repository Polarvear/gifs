FAQ

How do I convert PNG to GIF?

Click Browse Files (or drop images), ensure they are in the desired order, set seconds per frame and loop count, then click Convert to GIF. Everything is processed locally in your browser.

Is there a limit to the number of PNG files?

For smooth performance, try fewer than 50 frames and keep each image under ~5 MB. Actual limits depend on your device memory.

How long does it take?

Small images: seconds. Large images or many frames: longer. Encoding runs on your device using a web worker for responsiveness.

Will my images be uploaded?

No. This is a 100% client‑side tool; files never leave your browser.
